Tours

Explore the lush green landscape of Co. Laois following the Portlaoise Heritage Trail.

Immerse yourself in our history visiting Rock of Dunamase, Timahoe Round Tower, The Quaker town of Mountmellick and the planned town of Abbeyleix.
This tour includes transport and an amazing picnic.

PubCrawl - Because Pubs are an integral part of Irish culture and history

Embracing the concept of the "craic" (a Gaelic term meaning fun and good times), make new friends and enjoy an authentic night experience with good music, drinking games and away from the tourist crowds.
This is a walking tour for +18
